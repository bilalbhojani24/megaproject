Basic calculator

The most simple mathematical operations: addition, subtraction, multiplication, division. This is a simplified version of the online calculator. For the work of the simple calculator You can use both ordinary numeric buttons above the keyboard and separate numeric buttons on the right.

  • To delete the last character, press the [Backspace on keyboard] or [CE] key.
  • To enter a plus sign, use the [ + or on keyboard ] key at the top or on the side numeric keypad.
  • To enter a minus sign, use the [ - or on keyboard ] key at the top or on the side numeric keypad.
  • Use the [ * or on keyboard ] key on the side numeric keypad to enter the multiplication sign.
  • Use the [ / or on keyboard ] key on the side numeric keypad to enter the division sign.
  • You can use the [ C ] key to reset the calculator.
